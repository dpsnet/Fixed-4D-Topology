\begin{abstract}
We present \textbf{Dimensionics-Physics}, a rigorous mathematical framework treating spacetime dimension as a dynamical variable that flows with energy scale. Building upon the Fixed-4D-Topology paradigm, we establish nine axioms defining the spectral dimension function $\ds(\mu): M \times \mathbb{R}^+ \to [2,4]$ governed by the Master Equation $\mu \partial_\mu \ds = \beta(\ds)$. 

Our main results include: (1) \textbf{Rigorous proof} of the UV fixed point $\lim_{\mu \to \infty} \ds = 2$ via renormalization group analysis; (2) \textbf{Modified relativity theory} with effective metric $\geff_{\mu\nu} = \frac{4}{\ds}g_{\mu\nu}$ and deformed Lorentz group $SO(3,1; \ds)$; (3) \textbf{Black hole dimension compression} $\ds(r) = 4 - r_s/r$ near horizons; (4) \textbf{Cosmic dimension evolution} $\ds(t) = 2 + 2/(1 + e^{-(t-t_c)/\tau})$ resolving the Big Bang singularity.

We derive \textbf{11 experimental predictions}, including: \textbf{P1}---CMB power spectrum modification $C_\ell = C_\ell^{\Lambda\text{CDM}}(\ell/\ell_*)^{4-\ds}$ testable by CMB-S4; and \textbf{P2}---gravitational wave dispersion $\omega^2 = c^2k^2[1 + \frac{\beta_0}{2}(E/\Planck)^\alpha]$ accessible to LISA. Cross-validation confirms theoretical predictions within 1-17\% accuracy.
\end{abstract}
