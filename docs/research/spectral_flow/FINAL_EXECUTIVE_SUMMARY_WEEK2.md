## 核心数据

### 维度流动统一公式

$$d_{eff}(\epsilon, \chi) = d_\infty(\chi) + \frac{d_0 - d_\infty(\chi)}{1 + (\epsilon/\epsilon_c)^\alpha}$$

**参数**:
- $d_0 = 4.0$ (无约束)
- $d_\infty(\chi) = 2.5 - 0.5\chi$ (旋转依赖)
- $\epsilon_c = 0.8$, $\alpha = 1.7$
