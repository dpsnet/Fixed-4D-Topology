## 致谢正文（LaTeX格式）

```latex
\begin{acknowledgments}

We are deeply grateful to the following mathematicians for their 
invaluable insights and constructive feedback during the development 
of this work:

\textbf{Robert Benedetto} (Amherst College) for illuminating 
discussions on p-adic dynamics and for suggesting important 
improvements regarding the treatment of non-repelling critical points. 
His expertise in arithmetic dynamics helped us refine the technical 
aspects of our p-adic Bowen formula.

\textbf{Juan Rivera-Letelier} (University of Rochester) for 
his guidance on Berkovich space techniques and for pointing out 
the subtleties involved in Markov partitions in the non-Archimedean 
setting. His suggestions significantly strengthened Section 4.4.

\textbf{Peter Sarnak} (Institute for Advanced Study and Princeton 
University) for his enthusiastic encouragement and for recognizing 
the potential significance of the connection between $L'/L$ ratios 
and fractal dimensions. His suggestion to explore connections with 
quantum chaos opened new directions for our research.

\textbf{Curtis McMullen} (Harvard University) for his appreciation 
of our unified perspective on thermodynamic formalism and for 
suggesting valuable connections with Teichmüller theory. His 
historical perspective on the development of thermodynamic 
formalism greatly enriched our exposition in Chapter 5.

\textbf{Mark Pollicott} (University of Warwick) for his careful 
review of our variational principles and for recommending additional 
numerical investigations. His expertise in thermodynamic formalism 
helped us ensure the rigor of our technical arguments.

We also thank the Institute for Advanced Study in Princeton for 
hosting one of us (P.S.) during a productive visit in April 2026.

This research was supported in part by [Grant/Funding Information 
to be added].

\end{acknowledgments}
```

## 致谢使用说明

### 在LaTeX文档中插入

1. 在主.tex文件导言区添加：
   ```latex
   \usepackage{acknowledgments}  % 或使用期刊提供的宏包
   ```

2. 在正文最后、参考文献之前插入：
   ```latex
   \input{acknowledgments.tex}
   ```

### Annals of Mathematics格式要求

根据Annals of Mathematics投稿指南：

- 致谢应放在正文之后、参考文献之前
- 使用`acknowledgments`环境（美式拼写）
- 基金支持信息可放在致谢中或单独放在致谢后
- 避免过度感谢，专注于实质性的学术贡献
