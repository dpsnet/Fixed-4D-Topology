\Large\textbf{Suggested Reviewers}

\vspace{2em}

The following experts are suggested as potential reviewers for the manuscript ``Fractal Spectral Asymptotics and p-adic Thermodynamic Formalism: A Unified Framework for Kleinian Groups and Non-Archimedean Dynamics.'' These recommendations are based on their expertise in relevant mathematical fields.

\section*{Primary Recommendations}

\textbf{1. Professor Peter Sarnak}\\
School of Mathematics, Institute for Advanced Study\\
Princeton, NJ, USA\\
Email: sarnak@math.ias.edu\\
\textit{Expertise: Spectral theory, automorphic forms, quantum chaos}\\
\textbf{Reason:} World-renowned expert in spectral asymptotics and their connections to number theory and geometry.

\vspace{1.5em}

\textbf{2. Professor Curt McMullen}\\
Department of Mathematics, Harvard University\\
Cambridge, MA, USA\\
Email: ctm@math.harvard.edu\\
\textit{Expertise: Kleinian groups, complex dynamics, hyperbolic geometry}\\
\textbf{Reason:} Leading authority on Kleinian groups and their limit sets; Fields Medalist with deep expertise in the subject.

\vspace{1.5em}

\textbf{3. Professor Laura DeMarco}\\
Department of Mathematics, Harvard University\\
Cambridge, MA, USA\\
Email: demarco@math.harvard.edu\\
\textit{Expertise: Complex dynamics, arithmetic dynamics}\\
\textbf{Reason:} Expert in both complex and arithmetic dynamics; has made fundamental contributions to the study of dynamical moduli spaces.
